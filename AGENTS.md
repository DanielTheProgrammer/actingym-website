# ActinGym Website Agent Notes

## Product

ActinGym is an iOS-first AI acting coach for self-tape auditions.

Core promise:

> Upload your self-tape. Get director-level feedback in 2 minutes.

The product speaks like a director beside the actor: concrete, actable, and specific. Avoid vague coaching language such as "be more present" or "trust yourself."

## Visual Direction

- Brand-first, not generic SaaS-first.
- Warm editorial paper, rehearsal-room tactility, black self-tape gear, red recording light, green craft signal.
- Use real app/product visuals whenever possible.
- Keep the first viewport immersive and direct. Do not put the hero message inside a card.
- Avoid purple gradients, generic AI blobs, and abstract stock imagery.

## Implementation

- Use the existing fonts from the app: Fraunces, Plus Jakarta Sans, Caveat.
- Keep controls stable across breakpoints. Do not use viewport-width font scaling.
- Verify desktop and mobile after meaningful changes.
